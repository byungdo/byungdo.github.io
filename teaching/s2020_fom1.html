Term-paper presentation

Your term paper will be collected and you will be giving a presentation about your term paper. The presentation will take place on June 30th, 2020 19:00 at E1-1 Room 136 (the classroom we meet). You will be given 20 minutes to give a free-form presentation. The deadline for your term paper is also June 30th, 2020 19:00 and will be collected when your final exam is collected.

Term-paper policies: (1) Your term-paper must have a title An implementation of F. Klein's "Erlanger Programm" in secondary school mathematics curricula. That said, you do not have a freedom to choose the title. Of course your term-paper must have a contents that you can use the given title. There is no page limit or a format. You hand-in a paper that shows the depth and the width of your intellectual capacity. (2) You may use internet, books, and/or other resources. However, you must clarify the source in a distinguishable form. Failure in meeting this condition is called a plagiarism which is equivalent to a cheating in exam.
